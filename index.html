Hi, I'm Michelle.

A professional pet sitter based in Denver with 10 years of experience. I've worked at a doggy daycare, cared for pets of all ages and temperaments (including seniors and special-needs animals), and currently share my home with three crazy cats: Hades, Moose, and Onyx.

Animals have always been a part of my life. I grew up with pets and understand how important it is to find someone you truly trust. I believe every animal deserves care that feels familiar, calming, and full of love.

I work from home, which means I'm able to offer flexible, consistent, and attentive care based on each pet's unique needs and routine. Whether it's a midday drop-in, overnight stay, or companionship while you're away, I treat every pet like they're my own.
